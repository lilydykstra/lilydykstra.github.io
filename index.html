Welcome!

My name is Lily Dykstra. I am in my junior year student at the University of Colorado Boulder. I study Creative Technology and Design Engineering and minor in Mathematics. When I'm in as well as out of school I am creating, whether it's sewing patchwork bags from repurposed denim or creating wire and acrylic jewelry. I am passionate about the intersection of design and technology, with proficiency in Adobe Creative Cloud, Figma, and programming languages like Python and HTML. I work as a Prototype Designer for GFIex and CIRGO making 3D models and fabricated prototypes.

Currently, I am studying in Florence, Italy at Florence University of the Arts and the American University of Florence for the Fall 2024 semester.
